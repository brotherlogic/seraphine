# Deep Research Skill

When executing deep research tasks, evaluate the problem space thoroughly until at least the top 3 viable options are identified (this is the sufficiency threshold). 

You may organically decide how best to present the pros, cons, and tradeoffs of these options based on the specific problem space—there is no strict formatting template required. Provide enough detail for each option so that the tradeoffs are clear and an informed decision can be made.
