# Deep Research Skill

This skill instructs the agent to evaluate the problem space until at least the top 3 viable options are identified (which acts as the sufficiency threshold). 

## Instructions

1. Explore the problem space thoroughly.
2. Identify at least 3 viable options or solutions. This is the sufficiency threshold; you must not stop until you have at least 3 viable options.
3. Present the pros and cons of these options. You may decide organically how best to present this information (no strict formatting template is required).
