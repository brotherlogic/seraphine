# Deep Research Skill

When invoked, perform deep research on the given problem space. Follow these instructions:

1. Evaluate the problem space comprehensively until you have identified at least the top 3 viable options. This serves as the minimum sufficiency threshold for your research.
2. For the identified options, present the pros and cons of each.
3. You may decide organically how best to present this information (e.g., paragraphs, bullet points, comparisons); no strict formatting template is required. Focus on providing clear, detailed, and actionable insights.
