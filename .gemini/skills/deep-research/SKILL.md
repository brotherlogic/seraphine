# Deep Research Skill

When evaluating a problem space or conducting research, adhere to the following guidelines:

## Sufficiency Threshold
- You must evaluate the problem space until at least the **top 3 viable options** are identified. This is the minimum threshold required to complete the research task.

## Presentation of Findings
- Once the viable options are identified, you must present the pros and cons for each of them.
- You are free to decide organically how best to present these pros and cons—no strict formatting template is required. Focus on clarity and comprehensiveness based on what makes the most sense for the specific topic.
